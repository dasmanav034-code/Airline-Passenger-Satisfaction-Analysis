# ✈️ Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes airline passenger satisfaction data to uncover key factors influencing customer experience, loyalty, service quality, and operational performance. The dashboard transforms raw airline data into actionable business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking.

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze passenger satisfaction levels across different customer segments.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming airline services.
* Evaluate the impact of flight delays on customer satisfaction.
* Compare satisfaction trends by travel class, travel type, and age groups.
* Provide data-driven recommendations to improve customer experience.

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Strengths
* Cleanliness received the highest average rating.
* Baggage Handling showed strong customer satisfaction.
* Online Support performed consistently well.

### Improvement Areas
* Seat Comfort received the lowest rating.
* Food & Drink quality requires improvement.
* Gate Location received below-average ratings.

### Customer Insights
* Loyal customers account for the majority of satisfied passengers.
* Business travelers show higher satisfaction than personal travelers.
* Business Class passengers report better overall experiences.

### Operational Insights
* Flight delays negatively impact customer satisfaction.
* Long-distance travelers have higher service expectations.
* Passengers aged 31–45 represent the largest customer segment.
